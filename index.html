Hello, This is VR Theatre, a Virtual Reality Theatre made for having full dive theatre experience virtually.

Minimum requirements
RAM - 6 GB (Recommended 8 GB)
Processor - Snapdragon 845 + or similar
Display - 1080p

How to use
1. Click on the Hall at the top right corner of the page where you want to enter.
(Please be patient as it requires some time to load and let it render all the objects properly)

2. Move around your head to view the whole Theatre.

3. If you are using a desktop you can use W,A,S and D to move around.

4. The white Dot works as your focus button.

5. You can hold the white dot on the play button for 3 seconds to trigger it (or click if you are using any console).

Let's talk about this project

This project was created when everyone was locked in their home due to the global pandemic. We came up with the idea of creating a cross platform Virtual Reality Environment of a Theatre that can be used to have a full dive experience of a Cinema Hall right from the comfort of your own room.

Developed by Debtanu Goswami(The Website Designer and Builder) and Pranav Nautiyal (3d Model Artist) with Love.
